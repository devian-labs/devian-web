# Introducing Devian: The Control Center for Modern Developers

Modern development environments are complex. Between managing multiple microservices, keeping track of Docker containers, and debugging port conflicts, developers often feel like they're managing infrastructure instead of writing code.

Today, we're excited to announce the public beta of **Devian Desktop** — the all-in-one control center designed to bring clarity and intelligence to your local machine.

## Why Devian?

We built Devian because we noticed that developers operate in "hidden system complexity." Ports get blocked without explanation, disk space disappears mysteriously, and repositories are scattered across the filesystem.

Devian connects these dots into a unified interface.

### Unified Visibility
See your projects, ports, Docker containers, and disk usage in a single window. No more context-switching between the terminal, Activity Monitor, and Docker Desktop.

### Local AI Intelligence
Devian doesn't just show you data; it helps you understand it. Our local AI layer can explain why a port is blocked or suggest safe ways to reclaim gigabytes of disk space.

### Privacy First
Everything in Devian happens locally. Your data, system state, and AI reasoning never leave your machine.
